# RescueLink Privacy Policy (Plain Language)

*Last Updated: July 2026*

RescueLink is a real-time emergency care coordination platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, share, and protect your personal data in compliance with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023** and global healthcare safety standards.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect data that is critical to saving lives during an emergency dispatch or care context:
- **Emergency Telemetry**: Heart rate, oxygen levels, blood pressure, body temperature, and clinical notes.
- **Identity Information**: Patient Name, Date of Birth, Gender, and Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) ID.
- **Location Coordinates**: Real-time GPS location of the responding ambulance and patient pickup location.

---

## 2. Consent and Control (Your Rights)
Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have absolute control over your digital personal data:
- **Right to Access & Rectify**: You can request a summary of the personal data we hold and ask to correct any inaccuracies.
- **Right to Consent Withdrawal**: You can revoke consent for sharing your data with hospital networks at any time.
- **Right to Erasure (Forgotten)**: You can request the deletion of your personal records. Once approved, all identifiable data is permanently deleted from our active databases.

---

## 3. How We Share Data
We share your information strictly with:
- Authorized paramedics and ambulance staff responding to your SOS.
- The designated receiving hospital's clinical staff to prepare resources (e.g. ventilators, ICU beds).
- The national ABDM gateway if you authorize linking your care context.

---
